## 中文短介绍

`codex-skill-army` 是一个面向科研工作流的 Codex 单体 skill。它用“军制”统领用户本地已有和未来新增的 skills：总帅定目标，军师拆任务，斥候找缺口，主将攻核心，副将补能力，监军验质量，史官保留长期偏好。适合论文、文献综述、Word 学术排版、科研代码、Abaqus/FEA 和多代理协作。

## English Summary

`codex-skill-army` is a research-first orchestration protocol for OpenAI Codex skills. It does not replace domain skills; it coordinates installed and future skills through a clear role system, task decomposition, safe recruitment, subagent delegation, validation, and optional long-term research memory.
